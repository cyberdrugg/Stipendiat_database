Scholarship holders of INAI.KG 2022-23 academic year

The Kyrgyz-German Institute of Applied Informatics, together with the West-Saxon University of Applied Sciences (WHZ), Zwickau, implements scholarship programs through the DAAD service, both for undergraduate and graduate programs. The amount of an academic scholarship at INAI.KG allows you to fully or partially cover the cost of a student's education.
